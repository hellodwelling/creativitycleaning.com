About Us

Creativity Cleaning LLC was established by Dakeisha Seals out of the 2019 pandemic as housekeeping became a larger priority for families that could no longer leave the home. We provide cleaning services for residential homes, condos, manufactured homes, and commercial offices in Detroit, MI and surrounding areas. We offer a wide range of services such as move in move out cleaning, office cleaning, home cleaning, deep cleaning, and much more. FAQs

Your questions answered

What's the best way to reach you?

The best way to get in touch is to call or text (586) 623-6837. We provide personal attention to every customer.

How do I book my first appointment?

Simply call or text us (586) 623-6837, letting us know your cleaning requirements and the date that you need cleaning.

What is included in a standard cleaning?

Standard cleaning takes into account everything that involves cleaning a home, condo, apartment, etc. We will cleaning & vacuum your floors, clean your bathrooms, kitchen, living room, dusting, etc. We also offer additional services such as cleaning inside the fridge and inside the oven.

How do I refer a friend?

Share us with your friends! If a friend books through your referral, your friend automatically gets a $25 discount and you earn a $25 referral reward that can be applied to any future service. Just let us know your friends name!

What is your refund policy?

If something is not done to your expectations, please let us know. We’ll send one of our team members to make it right within 24 hours. If the cleaning is still not to your satisfaction, we'll provide you with a 50% refund.

Can I trust your cleaning professionals?

All of our team members are experienced and are required to pass a nationwide background check.

What is your cancellation policy?

Customers can make a cancellation or change up to 24 hours in advance of the schedule appointment. Cancellations made after this time will be charged a cancellation fee of $25.

What is your maximum hours?

With our flat-rate pricing model, we have a maximum amount of hours that can be worked for each type of job. We charge a rate of $35 / hr, for any job that exceeds 4 hours. We do this to account for jobs that require more intensive work or time due to size. It's rare to exceed the 4 hour mark, however if we think there is this possibility our team will let you know ahead of time.

Will your cleaning business show up on time?

We always do our best to show up on time, however occasionally unforeseen circumstances may lead to delays. We kindly ask that customers please allow us a 1-2 hour window to account for traffic, parking, and other surprises along the way.

What do I need to prepare before cleaning?

For move in and move out services, please kindly remove all furniture and personal belongings from the spaces that require cleaning. If you require cleaning around large appliances, please help us move them ahead of time. Due to liability reasons, our cleaners are not able to move these types of items.

If you are requesting deep cleaning, please help pick up clothing, personal items and clear up cluttered areas prior to our arrival.

Lastly, for any cleaning involving stain and mold removal, we cannot guarantee removal of all stains and mold on grout and tile inside the bathroom and kitchen.
